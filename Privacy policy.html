Privacy Policy

Aakhri Update: 18 February 2026

Hamari gaming website par aane ke liye shukriya. Aapki privacy hamare liye bahut zaroori hai.

1. Information Jo Hum Collect Karte Hain

Hum kisi bhi user ka personal data (jaise naam ya email) tab tak collect nahi karte jab tak aap humse contact na karein. Hum sirf basic technical data jaise IP address aur browser type collect karte hain taaki game experience ko behtar banaya ja sake.

2. Cookies

Hum cookies ka istemal karte hain taaki hum yaad rakh sakein ki aapne kaunsa game khela hai aur aapki settings kya hain. Aap apne browser ki settings se cookies ko band bhi kar sakte hain.

3. Bachon ki Privacy (Children's Privacy)

Hamare games sabhi umar ke logo ke liye hain. Hum jaan-boojh kar 13 saal se kam umar ke bacho se koi personal information nahi maangte.

4. Third-Party Ads (Google AdSense)

Hum ads dikhane ke liye third-party companies ka istemal kar sakte hain. Ye companies aapke visits ke bare mein jaankari ikhatta kar sakti hain taaki aapko aapke pasand ke ads dikhaye ja sakein.
